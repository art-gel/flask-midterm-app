Vegan Cinnamon Apple Muffins

Prep: 15 min | Bake: 20 min | Servings: 12 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients




Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Set aside egg replacer mixture for 5 minutes.
  3. Mix dry ingredients and save 1/4 cup of flour for the apples.
  4. Combine all of the ingredients.
  5. Pour into 12 cup muffin pan with liners.
  6. Sprinkle a mixture of cinnamon and sugar on top for extra crunch!
  7. Bake for 20 minutes and enjoy!
