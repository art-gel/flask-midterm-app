Notes

  • Egg replacer: 1 egg = 2 tbsp ground flaxseed + 3 tbsp water.
  • After 5+ minutes, the flaxseed mixture resemebles a gooey egg consistency.
  • It's more effecient to absorb Omega 3's and other vitamins with grounded flaxseed.
  • Remember to use canola oil! Vegetable oil unfortunately changes the flavor.
  • For a subtle sweetness and less overpowring flavor, choose Gala or Fuji apples.
  • Fair warning, these muffins are so delicious that they might not last for too long!.
